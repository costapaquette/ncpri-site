Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is NCPRI?

NCPRI is a nonprofit initiative formed to protect PhD candidates and ensure academic research integrity. We advocate for transparency, fairness, and student empowerment in higher education systems worldwide.

❓ Why was NCPRI founded?

NCPRI was created after multiple cases of institutional misconduct, negligence, and systematic failure of traditional student unions to protect research candidates. Our mission is to rebuild what was abandoned — independent, student-first protection and advocacy.

❓ Who can join NCPRI?

Any current or former PhD candidate, Master's by research student, postdoctoral researcher, or supporter of academic fairness can engage with NCPRI. Membership options will be formalized soon, with transparent pathways for involvement and contribution.

❓ How is NCPRI different from traditional student unions?

Unlike traditional unions, NCPRI is fully student-driven, nonprofit, transparent, and free from university control. Our sole loyalty is to students and research integrity, not institutional interests or funding deals.

❓ How can I support NCPRI?

You can support NCPRI by sharing our mission, joining our initiatives, donating to our future student defense fund, or volunteering your skills to build a stronger, safer academic future for all researchers.

❓ Where can I learn more?

Follow our official GitHub page, upcoming website launch, and LinkedIn posts. Regular updates, membership forms, advocacy reports, and resources will be made available as the movement grows.
