This guide explains how to encrypt your email securely before sending your submission to ncpri@protonmail.com. Encryption protects your identity and content from surveillance or interception.

Step 1: Download Our Public Key

Click here to download: public-key.asc

Step 2: Use a Free PGP Tool

We recommend one of the following tools:

  • ProtonMail (automatic encryption when sending to another ProtonMail address)
  • Encrypt.to (paste message + public key, get encrypted text)
  • PGPTool (downloadable GUI app for Windows/Linux/Mac)

Step 3: Compose Your Message

Write your full report, including details and attachments if needed. Then encrypt it using our public key.

Step 4: Send Encrypted Email

Send your encrypted message to ncpri@protonmail.com. We will only be able to read it using our matching private key.
